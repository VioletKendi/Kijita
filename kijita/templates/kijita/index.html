What is Kijita?

Kijita is a website for renting and selling your books. All of us have books we have read filling our bookshelves, why not rent them out? End your misery of buying brand new books, worry no more! Everyone gets a great value by either renting a book or listing books for hire.

How it works

Looking to earn money? Simply upload the books you would like to sell or rent out and we will do the rest.
If you are looking to hire or buy a pre-loved book, search for the book of your choice on our website and we shall recommend the best books and sellers at an affordable price.
It's that simple!
